# Velocity Platformer

An endless runner platformer where you roll through a neon world that gets faster the longer you survive.

## Play
[velocity platformer](https://aayan06.github.io/velocity_platformer/)

## Controls
- **Arrow keys / WASD** — move
- **Space / W / Up** — jump
- **Space while holding orb** — super jump

## How it works
The player automatically rolls forward and gradually picks up speed over time. The goal is to survive as long as possible by jumping over gaps, avoiding spikes and trees, and not running into walls. Reaching a score of 500 triggers a win.

Scattered throughout the level are glowing orbs. Collecting one stores a super jump — the next time you press jump it launches you much higher than normal. You can only hold one at a time.

The level loops seamlessly so the run never ends until you die.
